### Opens to new small window
```html
<a href="" target="_blank" onclick="return !window.open(this.href, '', 'toolbar=no, scrollbars=yes, resizable=no, status=no, menubar=no, location=no, top=300, left=300, width=400, height=400')">TEXT</a>
```
